Who We Are

Merging Technology with Artistry

Founded by a team of material scientists and dental professionals, Zirex began with a simple question: "Can we create a material that is as strong as metal but as beautiful as a natural tooth?"

Today, Zirex operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility utilizing Cold Isostatic Pressing (CIP) and nano-grade powder technology. Our products are ISO certified and trusted by over 500+ dental laboratories across the country.
