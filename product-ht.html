Zirex High Translucent (HT)

Contact for Dealer Pricing

Our High Translucent (HT) Zirconia is engineered specifically for the anterior region where esthetics are paramount. It combines superior transparency with adequate strength (1200 MPa), making it the ideal choice for veneers, inlays, onlays, and single crowns that mimic natural tooth enamel.

  • 45% Translucency for natural look
  • 1200 MPa Flexural Strength
  • Compatible with Amann Girrbach / 98mm Open Systems
  • Available in A1, A2, A3, B1 shades
  • Cold Isostatic Pressed (CIP) for uniform density
